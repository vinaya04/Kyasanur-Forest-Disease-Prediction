Precautions for Kyasanur Forest Disease (KFD)

Ticks that transmit Kyasanur Forest Disease

  • Avoid visiting forest areas during peak tick season (January to May).
  • Wear full-sleeved clothing and boots when visiting wooded areas.
  • Use tick repellents (e.g., DEET or permethrin) on exposed skin and clothes.
  • Get vaccinated if you live in or frequently visit endemic areas.
  • Regularly check for and remove ticks from body and clothing.
  • Inform health authorities if you notice monkey deaths nearby.
  • Stay alert to symptoms: fever, chills, body ache, and seek immediate medical help.
